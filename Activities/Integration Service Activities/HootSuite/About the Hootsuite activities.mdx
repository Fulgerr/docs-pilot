# About the Hootsuite activities

Hootsuite is a cloud-based social media management system. After connecting via the UiPath Connector for Hootsuite, you can manage and schedule posts to Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter and more.



To learn more about the Hootsuite activities, see the  page for a complete list and descriptions.

# How it works

To enable the outbound automation between UiPath and Hootsuite, the activities establish an authenticated connection using the UiPath Connector for Hootsuite. Before you build your first project, complete the steps in the Authentication guide from the connector documentation.
