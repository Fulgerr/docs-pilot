# Search Tasks

# Description

Search for tasks in Todoist based on keyword, priority, due
                date and assignee.

.

# Project Compatibility

Windows|Cross-Platform

# Configuration

* Connection - The connection established in Integration Service. Access the drop-down to choose, add or manage connections.
* Keyword - Search for tasks with a keyword. For example, to retrieve all tasks that contain the word incident that are due today, pass Meeting & today in this field.
* Priority - Search for tasks with priority. For example, to retrieve all tasks that are of the highest priority, pass p4.
* Due - Search for tasks by due date. For instance, to locate tasks due between today and next Thursday, you can use due after:today & due before: next thu. For tasks due on a specific date, you can use due: Dec 31.
* Assigned to - Find tasks assigned to others by providing their email or name. To find tasks assigned to you, just use me as the input in this field.
