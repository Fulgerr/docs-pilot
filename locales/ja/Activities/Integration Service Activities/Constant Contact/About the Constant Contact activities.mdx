# About the Constant Contact activities

Constant Contact is a web-based email marketing platform. After connecting, you can bring powerful automation to email marketing campaigns and related processes—from content creation and management to execution and reporting.



To learn more about the Constant Contact activities, see the Activities page for a complete activity list and descriptions.

# How it works

To enable the outbound automation between UiPath and Constant Contact, the activities establish an authenticated connection using the UiPath connector for Constant Contact.

Before you build your first project, complete the steps in the Authentication guide from the connector documentation.
