# About the HubSpot CRM activities

HubSpot CRM is a customer relationship management platform noted for its powerful features that enable customers to relate stronger with their customers, boost conversations, and improve revenues based on multiple interaction channels.



To learn more about the HubSpot CRM activities, see the Activities page for a complete list and descriptions.

# How it works

To enable the outbound automation between UiPath and HubSpot, the activities establish an authenticated connection via the HubSpot CRM Connector. Before you build your first project, complete the steps in the Authentication guide from the connector documentation.
